# Export Workflow Checklist

## Before Documentation Preparation

* Verify customer purchase order
* Confirm product specifications
* Confirm shipment schedule
* Verify destination country requirements

## Documentation Preparation

* Commercial Invoice
* Packing List
* Shipping Instructions
* Certificate Requirements

## Verification

* Product descriptions verified
* Quantity verified
* Weight verified
* Currency verified

## Final Review

* Internal approval completed
* Documentation consistency verified
* Shipment readiness confirmed
